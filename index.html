The Benefits of using AI to do schoolwork.

Usually in schools, AI is looked at as a way to cheat on a test or an assignment.
But when it is used properly as a tool to answer specific questions or solve simple solutions,
it can be very useful. Not all use of AI is to gain an unfair advantage in school. It can help in
many different situations, such as answering a question or giving prompts or ideas to write about
from a specific topic.
